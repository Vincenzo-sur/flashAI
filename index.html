What this app will do

Three things, done well — one for the admin, one for students, one for the teacher.

🧠
Generate flashcards from transcript

A class admin pastes the day's transcript. Gemini AI reads it and generates flashcards — each with a question, a clear answer, and 4 MCQ options.

📝
Students review & rate

Students answer the MCQ on each card, then self-rate: Know it, Fuzzy, or Don't know. Both signals together give a fuller picture of comprehension.

📊
Teacher sees weak & strong topics

The teacher's dashboard shows which topics were understood well and which ones need re-teaching — based on combined MCQ scores and self-ratings from the whole class.
